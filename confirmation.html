Brief Analysis

The client aims to develop an educational mobile app called Lighti – Kids' STEM Adventure, combining learning and fun through a character-driven experience. The character, Lighti, acts as a playful mentor guiding children (ages 5–10) through physics, mechanics, and electricity concepts using interactive challenges.

The client has already developed the core concept and character design and outlined the educational mechanics. They seek to launch a lightweight MVP within 2–3 months, focusing on lean development due to a limited budget.

The app should have a bright, cartoonish design with simple navigation, be scalable for future content expansion, and maintain a balance between fun and learning. No deep integrations are required at the MVP stage. Basic animations of Lighti are requested to bring the character to life.

Fast, cost-efficient development is critical, with a focus on creating an intuitive, engaging experience that can gather initial feedback from parents and educators for future improvements.
